Headings

How we write headings, and maintain structure and consistency.

Headings are split into 3 categories: homepage headers, dashboard headers, and page titles.

Homepage headers: aim for short passive nominalised terms, for example:

  • Finance
  • Apprentices
  • PAYE schemes
  • Your team


Dashboard headers: aim for active phrases, for example:

  • View your finances
  • Download financial data
  • Add an apprentice
  • Manage your apprentices


Page titles: aim for active phrases relating to the specific action on that page wherever possible, for example:

  • Confirm your identity
  • Enter your access code
  • Add an organisation
  • Check details
